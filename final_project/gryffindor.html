What Makes a Gryffindor?

Gryffindor’s are the embodiment of their house symbol, the brave lion, in this house you will find the most spirited and most daring students. The house colors are scarlet and gold, the common room lies up in Gryffindor Tower and the Head of House is Professor Minerva McGonagall.

Demonstrating qualities like courage, bravery and determination, are most certainly the reason the Sorting Hat would have placed you in this house. Some of the wizarding world’s best and brightest belonged to this house – some notable members being Hermione Granger, Harry Potter and Albus Dumbledore.

If you so happen to end up in Gryffindor, we imagine you’re the type of person who likes to stand up for what is right, challenges authority, can be a bit intrepid, are known for being a comedian and a jokester and always up for a competition.
