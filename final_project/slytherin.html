What Makes a Slytherin?

Despite Slytherin’s unfortunate reputation of producing dark witches and wizards, not all who belong to this house are bad. In fact, there are a lot of excellent qualities the Sorting Hat looks for in potential Slytherins, such as, ambition, cunningness, and resourcefulness. Merlin himself even belonged to this misunderstood house!

Slytherin’s can be seen around campus sporting crisp silver and deep emerald green robes embroidered with their house emblem the serpent. The Head of House is Professor Severus Snape, and the common room can be found down in the dungeons under the lake.

Those placed in this noble house by the Sorting Hat, tend to be determined, keen and possibly destined for greatness. You’re the kind of person who is always ahead of the game, has a dark sense of humor, cares about your character, presentation is key for you and you rarely let anyone see your soft side.
